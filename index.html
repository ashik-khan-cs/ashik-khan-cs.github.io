Comparative Analysis of Resource-Efficient CNN Architectures for Brain Tumor Classification

Md Ashik Khan, Rafath Bin Zafar Auvee

2024 27th International Conference on Computer and Information Technology (ICCIT), Cox's Bazar, Bangladesh, pp. 639-644

Cited by 12

Accurate brain tumor classification in MRI images is essential for timely diagnosis and effective treatment planning. Despite its lower complexity, our custom CNN architecture achieved 98.67% accuracy on the Br35H dataset and 99.62% on the Brain Tumor MRI Dataset, showing competitive performance against pre-trained ResNet18 and VGG16 models.
