About Me

Greetings! I am Md Ashik Khan, a passionate individual with a profound interest in the fields of deep learning, computer vision, and machine learning. My academic journey has been enriched by my recent completion of post-graduation from the department of Computer Science and Engineering at IIT Kharagpur (CGPA: 8.76/10.00).

Before delving into advanced studies, I accumulated valuable industry experience as a Software Development Engineer, working for two years in the tech industry. During this time, I contributed to the development of ERP and accounting applications, leveraging the power of React JS and Laravel.

During my post-graduation, I had the privilege of conducting a large-scale study in Representational Learning and Benchmarking in Video Action Recognition under the guidance of Dr. Abir Das. Additionally, I served as a teaching assistant, supporting a class of 160 students, and as a research assistant at the CVIR Lab.

Throughout my academic tenure, I undertook various projects, including "Context Specific Quote Recommendation from Historical Text" and "Evidence Retrieval for Fact Verification," which provided valuable insights into information retrieval and natural language processing. I am deeply fascinated by the potential of these fields to drive innovation and solve complex real-world problems.
